Pet Facilities

Here at Pet Hotel we provide a safe place for your pets while you are away.

Pet Hotel can arrange to have your dog groomed during their stay with us. Whether it is a full groom or just a wash & dry you require we can arrange to have it done prior to departure from the kennel.

We can provide bedding, toys etc. we encourage pet owners to bring their own should they wish to, along with any comforters as we have found that it helps pets to settle in quicker when they have familiar belongings with them.

We can arrange video calls between the pets and their owners, so you won’t miss your furry friend too much.

OWe also provide a CCTV System to enable the hotel staff to keep a watchful eye on all pets from a monitoring room.

We have also found that pets benefit from continuing their usual diet therefore we ask owners bring any special dietary food for the duration of their pets stay. Any instructions given regarding feeding routine will be adhered to and will be catered for.

We also think it’s a good idea to introduce pets to each other as some pets as be territorial.

Please note that we can also administer any medication required.

Owners Declaration

  • An animal is accepted for boarding by the proprietor on the condition that all its inoculations are up to date for either cat or dog or both.
  • The dog must have its kennel cough vaccine, no later than one week prior to boarding and is free from fleas and parasites.
  • The expenses for any veterinary attention that may be considered necessary during the animals stay, shall be reimbursed to the proprietor by the owner of the animal on the date agreed for collection.
  • All reasonable steps will be taken to ensure the safety and wellbeing of your pet during its stay, however no responsibility or liability of any kind for any accident or misadventure that may befall your animal shall be held to attach to the proprietor, owners or occupiers of these premises.
  • Please state the history of the dog/cat. please inform Pet Hotel of your pet's disposition.
  • It is the policy of Pet Hotel to kennel each dog/cat separately, should a client require two or more dog/cats to be kennelled together we will accommodate accordingly, but Pet Hotel accepts no liability or responsibility for any injuries, loss or damage caused to/by the animal.
  • It is the policy of Pet Hotel to only return the dog/cat to the owner who dropped off their pet. If for some reason the owner cannot collect their pet and intends to send somebody else to do so, we require confirmation from the owner either by text or email that they have authorised a named person to collect either their dog/cat. In the absence of such confirmation, we can only return the dog/cat to the owner.
